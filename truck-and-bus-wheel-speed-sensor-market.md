<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck and Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market consists of two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation and provide accurate speed data, making them reliable for real-time monitoring. Conversely, Magnetic Electric sensors operate on the principle of electromagnetic induction, generating a voltage signal as the wheel turns. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle safety, improving stability control systems, and facilitating anti-lock braking systems in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
