<p><p>The Arc-Fault Circuit Interrupter (AFCI) market includes several types: Branch/Feeder AFCI, Combination AFCI (CAFCI), and others. Branch/Feeder AFCIs protect specific circuits from arc faults and are typically installed in electrical panels. Combination AFCIs, on the other hand, provide enhanced protection by detecting both series and parallel arcs, making them suitable for branch circuits. Other variants may include portable AFCIs and specialized types for unique applications. These devices are essential for preventing electrical fires and enhancing overall safety in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
