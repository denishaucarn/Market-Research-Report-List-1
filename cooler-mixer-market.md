<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooler mixers are categorized into two main types based on their design orientation: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal mixers, commonly used in industrial applications, provide efficient mixing through a cylindrical shape, allowing gravity to assist in blending materials. Conversely, vertical mixers utilize a vertical design that promotes effective mixing of bulk materials through a central agitator, ideal for smaller batches or space-constrained environments. Both types cater to varying production needs in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
