<p><p>The splitter gearbox market is divided into two primary types: single pump drive and multi pump drive. Single pump drive systems use one pump to power the gearbox, providing a straightforward and efficient solution for various applications. In contrast, multi pump drive setups integrate multiple pumps to distribute power across several outputs, enhancing flexibility and performance in complex systems. Both types cater to different operational needs, with single pump drives being simpler and multi pump drives offering greater capacity and adaptability.</p></p>
