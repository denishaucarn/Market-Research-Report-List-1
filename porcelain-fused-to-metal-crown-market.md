<p><p>The Porcelain-Fused-to-Metal (PFM) crown market encompasses three primary types based on metal substrate: titanium, base-metal alloy, and noble metal alloy. Titanium crowns are known for their biocompatibility and strength, making them suitable for patients with metal allergies. Base-metal alloys, commonly including nickel and chromium, are cost-effective with strong properties but may cause allergic reactions. Noble metal alloys, containing gold or palladium, offer excellent aesthetics and durability but come at a higher price point, appealing to patients seeking quality and longevity.</p></p>
