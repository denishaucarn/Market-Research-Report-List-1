<p><strong>The Building Asphalt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-Modified Bitumen</li><li>Bitumen Emulsion</li><li>Cutback Emulsion</li><li>Oxidized Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building asphalt market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Polymer-modified bitumen enhances flexibility and durability, making it ideal for heavy traffic surfaces. Bitumen emulsions are water-based solutions used in cold applications for road construction and maintenance. Cutback emulsions contain solvents that promote quick setting and workability. Oxidized emulsions, produced by controlled air-blowing, offer superior waterproofing and aging properties, making them suitable for roofing and waterproofing applications. Each type caters to distinct performance and environmental needs.</p></p>
