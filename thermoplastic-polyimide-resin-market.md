<p><p>The thermoplastic polyimide resin market is categorized into two main types: extrusion grade and molding grade. Extrusion grade is designed for high-performance applications, offering excellent flow properties for continuous processing, ideal for films, coatings, and complex shapes. Molding grade, on the other hand, is tailored for injection molding processes, providing superior strength, thermal stability, and resistance to chemicals. Both grades cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, electronics, and automotive, due to their unique properties and versatility in applications.</p></p>
