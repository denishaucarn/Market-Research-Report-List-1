<p><p>The diabetic care market encompasses three main types: Type I, Type II, and Gestational Diabetes. Type I diabetes, often referred to as youth-onset diabetes, typically develops in children and young adults, requiring lifelong insulin management. Type II diabetes, or adult-onset diabetes, usually appears in adults and is often linked to lifestyle factors, involving medication and lifestyle changes for management. Gestational diabetes occurs during pregnancy, requiring close monitoring and treatment to ensure the health of both mother and child. Each type necessitates tailored care strategies.</p></p>
