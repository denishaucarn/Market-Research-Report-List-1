<p><p>The P2 hybrid module market includes two primary types: P2 on-axis drive modules and P2 off-axis drive modules. On-axis drive modules integrate the electric motor coaxially with the transmission, optimizing space and efficiency in vehicle design. In contrast, off-axis drive modules position the electric motor separately from the transmission, enabling greater flexibility in design and allowing for unique configurations. Both types cater to diverse automotive applications, contributing to the growing demand for electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
