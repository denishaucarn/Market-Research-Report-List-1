<p><p>The heavy-duty truck steering system market includes three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which uses hydraulic fluid to assist steering, enhancing maneuverability under heavy loads; Electric Power Steering (EPS), which relies on electric motors for steering assistance, offering greater energy efficiency and reduced weight; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines both hydraulic and electric systems for optimized performance and better fuel efficiency.
