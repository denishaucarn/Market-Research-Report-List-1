<p><p>The silver metallization paste market comprises two main types: front side Ag paste and rear side Ag paste. Front side Ag paste is primarily used for creating conductive patterns on the solar cell's front surface, enhancing light absorption and overall efficiency. In contrast, rear side Ag paste is applied to the back surface, improving electrical conductivity and reducing recombination losses. Together, these pastes are crucial for optimizing solar cell performance and ensuring efficient energy conversion in photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
