<p><p>The Barrier CMP Slurry Market comprises two primary types: Copper Barrier Slurry and Tantalum Barrier Slurry. Copper Barrier Slurry is designed for chemical-mechanical polishing of copper interconnects in semiconductor devices, facilitating smooth and defect-free surfaces. Tantalum Barrier Slurry, on the other hand, is utilized for polishing tantalum films, crucial for preventing copper diffusion in advanced semiconductor applications. Both slurries play vital roles in enhancing the performance and reliability of microelectronic components, catering to the growing demands of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
