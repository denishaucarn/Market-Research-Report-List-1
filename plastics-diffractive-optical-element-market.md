<p><p>The Plastics Diffractive Optical Element (DOE) market comprises various types, including beam shaping, beam splitting, and beam focusing. Beam shaping elements transform light into specific intensity profiles, such as top-hat distributions, enhancing uniformity. Beam splitting elements divide light into multiple paths for applications like imaging and sensing. Beam focusing elements concentrate light into a small area, improving energy delivery in laser systems. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like telecommunications, medical devices, and industrial processing.</p></p>
